Grocery stores are essential businesses

Everyone buys groceries. What products do customers purchase most often? Which category is their favorite?
Let's take a look at the data visualizations based on Groceries Dataset from Kaggle.

From the chart, we can see that whole milk is bought most often. And four of them are fresh produce. Others include diary products, meat, and beverages. From common sense, these are pretty much what we expect people to buy when doing grocery shopping.

What else do people love to buy?

Let us look at a packed bubble chart with 100 most purchased products.
Click on any bubble to display another bar chart showing details of the corresponding category.

The categories are manually assigned, so they might be inaccurate. From the chart, we can see that fresh produce and diary products are purchased most often.

When do people go grocery shopping more often?

Let's see an area chart of numbers of products sold for each day from the beginning of 2014 to the end of 2015.


We can see a lot of peaks here, but they only represent the number of products purchased that day. What if we look at the number of unique customers by day of the week and by month?
